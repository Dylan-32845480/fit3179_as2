Air Travel in Malaysia

Air travel is essential part of connecting people across Malaysia. With vast oceans separating its regions, many rely on flights as their main form of transport between regions. This visualization highlights the key aspects of air travel within Malaysia.

Airport Traffic

Many people come to and from Malaysia through the airports, making them vital gateways for international and domestic travel. The following map illustrates the total passenger traffic handled by various airports, showcasing the magnitude and significance of air travel in different cities.

Passenger Types

Not every passenger handled by the airports are here for the same purpose. Some passengers may be locals travelling for work while others may be international passengers arriving for holiday. The following graph focuses on the distribution of passenger types by airport to showcase the proportions of domestic and international passengers.

Trends in Air Travel

As time goes on, air travel frequency may change due to world events and evolving economic conditions. The following graph focuses on the change in air travel over time to highlight how air travel has adapted and transformed over the years.

Flight Traffic

There are many flight paths one can take to reach a destination and as such, the number of passengers that travel these flight paths may not always be equal. This following map shows the different flight paths between Malaysian airports and how frequently they are traveled to showcase the most popular and heavily trafficked routes.
